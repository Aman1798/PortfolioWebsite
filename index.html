About Me

Hello! I'm Amandeep Singh, deeply committed to advance and grow in the realms of Data Science and fintech. I am dedicated to expanding my Data Science expertise, I am a Master of Science in Data Science graduate from King's College London. Additionally, I hold a Bachelor's degree in Economics (Honors) from the University of Delhi.

In my academic journey, I have developed proficiency in various programming languages and tools including Python, R, SQL, Tableau, SPSS etc. Additionally, I have completed courses in Statistical methods for Economics, Data Mining, Machine Learning, and other Data Science and finance related subjects.

I have gained professional experience through Data Science Internships at New Motion Labs and British Airways, where I applied my expertise in data analysis and machine learning to develop NML's backend licensing portal and extracted insights from customer review data to build predictive models. Before that role, I served as a Research Analyst, specializing in analyzing ESG and pre-IPO data of S&P 500 companies.

I have also contributed to academic projects such as Golf Winnings, where I utilized linear regression to predict average total winnings per tournament, and Classification of Iris flower, where I developed a machine learning classification model with a 96% accuracy rate.

Beyond my academic and professional commitments, I find pleasure in delving into open data APIs, including the Ethereum API and Covid Data API etc, leveraging analytical techniques to extract and derive insights.

In addition to my academic and professional endeavors, I am honored to have received Scholar Badge Award and have recently, been awarded the Enterprise Award bestowed by King's Entrepreneurship Institute.
